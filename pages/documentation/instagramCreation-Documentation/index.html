Choosing a topic

I'm a huge fan of snowboarding and mountain sports in general. Whenever I'm in the mountains I find myself with joy, especially when I'm in the snowy rocky mountains. Because of that, I naturally gravitated towards Snowboarding üèÇ! This is why I chose to make a snowboarding themed instagram account to learn about different content creation methods. This way it made it easier for someone like me who doesn't use social media much.

So, I have chosen my instagram accounts theme and had to come up with various post ideas that were unique. There is so many ways to make instagram posts, on all sorts of levels. You could make very high quality media files and post them to instagram, which makes the over all account more impactful in my opinion. Another solution is to purely make all your content via the mobile device you're using, which is the way I did all the instagram posts with some help for one post utalizing Photoshop. Each post was thought about and seen through, with my own twist of what I was trying to accomplish.

Conclusion

I want to reiterate the fact that I'm not an active social media user, however I do know how impactful social media is. That is due to me working on this project and looking at other instagram accounts, I witnessed the type of immersive authoring that happens to engage with audiences. I learned that there is a variety of ways to going about content creation for Instagram specifically, and the limits I feel like are your creativity and access to tools for creating digital media. For this project, I only utalized photoshop once and used my mobile device with apps for every other post that I made. But I think if I used professional apps on my desktop I could probably produced high quality content.

To sum up my overall lesson of this project is that Immersive Authoring also envelopes what content can be published to Instagram. I believe utalizing any tools you have access to help with the production process will help, however I recommend using any professional digital media applications--like adobe applications.
