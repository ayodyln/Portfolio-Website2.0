Choosing a topic

I'm a huge fan of snowboarding and mountain sports in general. Whenever I'm in the mountains I find myself with joy, especially when I'm in the snowy rocky mountains. Because of that, I naturally gravitated towards Snowboarding üèÇ! This is why I chose to make snowboarding themed Instagram account to learn about different content creation methods. This way it made it easier for someone like me who doesn't use social media much.

So, I have chosen my Instagram accounts theme and had to come up with various unique post ideas. There are so many ways to make Instagram posts, on all sorts of levels. You could make very high-quality media files and post them to Instagram, which makes the overall account more impactful in my opinion. Another solution is to purely make all your content via the mobile device you're using, which is the way I did all the Instagram posts with some help for one post utilizing Photoshop. Each post was thought about and seen through, with my twist of what I was trying to accomplish.

Instagram Post Creation

Each post I did utilize my iPhone 12 and Ipad Pro (5th Gen.), and Photoshop was used throughout this project. I used the Instagram app to make some custom posts, I also used another app that helped format layouts with images, and the camera app on my Ipad to capture high-quality video.

Slow Motion Post

For this post, I wanted to share a cool tool that anyone can make at home. They only need to buy a 2 liter soda bottle and skateboard deck that is comfortable to use. All you need is to fill the bottle with water and roll the deck over the bottle, this is a make-shift balance board. Which helps you practice balance and its fun to play around with! I utalized my Ipad to capture a slow motion video and I edited it to my liking. This was from testing the video by watching what was being trimmed or not trimmed. I wrote the caption based off of what my audience might best get out of my post. So, I mention the DIY balance board.

Mountain Image Post

Here is a post that is pretty simple, but images can be pretty impactful alone. I took this photo on the mountain at the Brighton Resort in Utah. I just simply had to write a caption that just expressed my love for Brighton and the views you get their. I took a few pictures so I had a bunch to choose from, but I also had to decide if I wanted to doctor the image in any way possible. I added a black and white filter to the image, this was inspired by some snowboard photographers that used authentic cameras that shoot in black and white.

Conclusion

I want to reiterate the fact that I'm not an active social media user, however, I do know how impactful social media is. That is due to being working on this project and looking at other Instagram accounts, I witnessed the type of immersive authoring that happens to engage with audiences. I learned that there is a variety of ways to go about content creation for Instagram specifically, and the limits I feel like are your creativity and access to tools for creating digital media. For this project, I only utilized photoshop once and used my mobile device with apps for every other post that I made. But I think if I used professional apps on my desktop I could probably produce high-quality content.

To sum up, my overall lesson of this project is that Immersive Authoring also envelopes what content can be published to Instagram. I believe utilizing any tools you have access to help with the production process will help, however, I recommend using any professional digital media applications--like adobe applications.
