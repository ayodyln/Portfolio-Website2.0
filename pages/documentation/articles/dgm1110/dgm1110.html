PhotoShop

Image Manipulation

The manipulation of images is what Photoshop is best at. The the example image above is stitched together with two images and some color correction to try to capture the ambient light appropriately in the foreground. The original image was a picture of Earth from orbit and the second is of a girl sitting in a window looking at the city skyline.

Photoshop Painting

I've always been fascinated by how people can digitally paint beautiful scenes within Photoshop. So I wanted to attempt that. It isn't the best example of a Photoshop painting. However, I tried my best to capture the depth and done with only the brush tool.

Illustrator

Logo style guide made with Illustrator

Creating a logo takes a lot of effort and iteration. Logo makers can define a brand just by a logo. My example isn't the best of what is out there, but it's an example of how to come up with a style guide for a logo and how to present it. This was all done with Illustrator since it is great for graphics for logos or vector files.

Audition (Audio)

Audition is an application that helps edit audio tracks. My the example above is of the new Hogwarts Legacy game and I cut out the audio of that original trailer footage. Using audio clips, I tried to add ambient noises that synced with actions found within each scene.

Premiere (Video)

Using Premiere, I edited a video recording I made while playing a match of League of Legends. The goal was to capture highlights and use transitions to make a short video. I was able to understand how to manipulate video clips because of this.

After Effects (Animation)

Recently, I got chicks to raise and it was perfect timing for the lessons about After Effects. I wanted to try to headtrack a chick because they move around a lot in random patterns. It felt like a great challenge and I now appreciate this app more now.
